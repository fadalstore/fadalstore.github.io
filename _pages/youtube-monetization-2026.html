1. YouTube Partner Program (YPP) Requirements 2026

To access AdSense and most official monetization features, you need to join the YouTube Partner Program (YPP). Here are the current requirements:

TierRequirementsBenefits
YPP Basic500 subs + 3,000 watch hours OR 3M Shorts views (90 days)Channel memberships, Super Thanks, Super Chat, Super Stickers
YPP Standard1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours OR 10M Shorts views (90 days)AdSense revenue + all Basic features

⚡ Important: You Don't Need YPP for All Methods

Methods 5–12 below work even before you qualify for YPP. Affiliate marketing, digital products, and brand deals are available from Day 1 with any subscriber count. Many creators earn $1,000+/month before reaching 1,000 subscribers.

2. All 12 YouTube Monetization Methods

METHOD 1

▶️ AdSense (YouTube Ad Revenue)

The most well-known method. YouTube shows ads on your videos and pays you 55% of ad revenue. Earnings vary by niche: Finance pays $15–$50 CPM, Entertainment pays $1–$3 CPM. Requires YPP Standard membership.

$100–$50,000+/month Medium difficulty
METHOD 2

🤝 Brand Sponsorships

Companies pay you to mention their product in your video. This is often 3–5x more lucrative than AdSense. A channel with 50,000 subscribers in a good niche can charge $500–$2,000 per sponsored mention. Use platforms like Grapevine, AspireIQ, or email brands directly.

$200–$50,000/video Works at 10K+ subs
METHOD 3

👥 Channel Memberships

Fans pay $4.99–$49.99/month for exclusive perks (badges, emojis, exclusive content). If 500 fans pay $9.99/month, that's $4,995/month in recurring income. Requires 1,000+ subscribers and YPP Basic.

$500–$20,000/month Needs loyal audience
METHOD 4

💬 Super Chat, Super Thanks & Super Stickers

Viewers pay to pin their messages during live streams (Super Chat) or to highlight their comments on regular videos (Super Thanks). Gaming, cooking, and reaction channels earn heavily from this. Top creators earn $10,000+/month just from Super Chat.

$200–$10,000/month Best with live content
METHOD 5

🔗 Affiliate Marketing (Best for Beginners!)

Recommend products in your video and drop your affiliate link in the description. Every purchase earns you 5%–50% commission. Amazon Associates, PartnerStack, ShareASale, and Impact are top networks. A review channel with 5,000 views/video can earn $500–$3,000/month this way.

$300–$15,000/month Works from Day 1
METHOD 6

📚 Digital Products (Courses, eBooks, Templates)

Sell your own product to your audience. A YouTube course selling at $97 and sold to 50 people/month = $4,850/month. Use Gumroad, Teachable, or Kajabi. Your YouTube audience already trusts you — they're the most likely buyers.

$500–$50,000/month High effort, high reward
METHOD 7

👕 Merchandise

Sell branded merchandise: t-shirts, hoodies, mugs, phone cases. YouTube has a built-in merch shelf that shows products below your video (requires 10,000 subscribers). Also use Printful, Printify, or Spring for no-inventory print-on-demand merch.

$200–$10,000/month Needs personal brand
METHOD 8

🎵 YouTube Shorts Fund & Shorts Ads

YouTube Shorts (vertical videos under 60 seconds) now share ad revenue just like long-form videos. Short-form content can reach millions of views quickly. Many creators build audiences of 100K+ subscribers in months purely through Shorts, then monetize with all methods above.

$50–$5,000/month Easy to start
METHOD 9

🏆 YouTube Premium Revenue

YouTube Premium subscribers ($13.99/month) see no ads. YouTube distributes part of their subscription fee to channels they watch. If Premium members watch your content, you earn extra on top of AdSense. Automatically applied — no action needed.

$50–$2,000/month extra Automatic bonus
METHOD 10

🎓 Coaching & Consulting

Use YouTube to demonstrate your expertise, then offer 1-on-1 coaching. A coach charging $200/session and booking 10 sessions/month earns $2,000 — from clients who found them on YouTube. This works in fitness, business, finance, relationships, and any niche.

$1,000–$20,000/month High value
METHOD 11

📰 Newsletter / Email List Monetization

Use YouTube to build an email list. Send weekly emails with affiliate offers, product recommendations, or your own products. An email list of 5,000 people can earn $1,000–$5,000/month through promotions. Email lists are owned by you — unlike YouTube subscribers, they can't be taken away.

$500–$10,000/month Long-term asset
METHOD 12

🏢 Licensing & Content Syndication

News channels, TV shows, and media companies often pay to license viral footage. If you capture a unique event on video, media companies may pay $500–$5,000 to use it. Register with Jukin Media or ViralHog to license your content. Also: sync your YouTube content to Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram Reels for additional platform payments.

$100–$5,000/video Opportunity-based

5. Getting Paid From Somalia / Hargeysa

💳 YouTube Payment Methods Available in Somalia

YouTube pays via wire transfer. To receive USD in Somalia:

  • Wise (best option) — Get a US/EU bank account, link to YouTube AdSense, withdraw to Wise, then transfer to Dahabshiil or EVC Plus/ZAAD
  • Payoneer — Also works, but slightly higher fees than Wise
  • Minimum payout threshold: $100
  • Payments sent monthly between the 21st–26th of each month

✅ Somalia-Specific Tip

Create content in English targeting global audiences for maximum AdSense income. Somali-language content can grow fast locally, but English content has 100x more AdSense advertisers and higher CPMs. Best strategy: make English main content, add Somali subtitles.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How many views do I need to make $1,000/month from YouTube?
For AdSense alone: roughly 200,000–500,000 views/month depending on your niche CPM. But with affiliate marketing and sponsorships, you can make $1,000/month with just 10,000–20,000 views/month in a good niche.
❓ Can I use YouTube from Somalia and get paid?
Yes. Use a Wise or Payoneer account linked to your AdSense to receive payments. You need to verify your identity and address. Many Somali creators earn from YouTube monthly using this method.
❓ Do I need to show my face on YouTube?
No. Faceless channels using screen recordings, animations, stock footage, and AI voiceovers are very popular and profitable. Finance, history, meditation, and tutorial channels often use no on-camera presenter.
❓ How often should I post on YouTube?
Consistency beats frequency. 1 high-quality video per week is better than 5 low-quality videos. For Shorts, 1–3 per day is effective for rapid growth. The algorithm rewards consistency over time.
❓ What's the minimum subscribers for sponsorships?
You can get brand deals at any subscriber count if your engagement is high. Micro-influencers (1,000–10,000 subscribers) can get $50–$500 per sponsored video in the right niche. At 100,000 subscribers, rates jump to $2,000–$10,000+.
