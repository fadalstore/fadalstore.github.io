2. New Seller — The Critical First Steps

🆕 NEW SELLER

New Seller Status

  • No orders required to start
  • Can create up to 7 gigs immediately
  • Complete your profile (photo, bio, skills, language)
  • Add portfolio samples even before first order
  • Set competitive prices to attract first buyers

Goal: Get your first 10 orders with 5-star reviews as fast as possible.

🎯 New Seller Profile Optimization Checklist

  • Professional profile photo (clear face, good lighting)
  • Compelling bio (describe your skill + who you help + results they get)
  • Skills section filled with relevant tags
  • At least 3 portfolio samples (even practice work)
  • Education and certifications added
  • Online status set to active (respond within 1 hour when possible)
  • Gig video added — sellers with video gigs get 220% more clicks

3. Level 1 Seller Requirements 2026

⭐ LEVEL 1

Level 1 Seller Requirements

  • 90% on-time delivery rate (last 60 days)

Benefits of Level 1:

  • ✅ Offer up to 2 gig extras (add-ons)

7. How to Level Up Fast — 10 Proven Tips

⚡ Tip 1: Respond Within 1 Hour

Response rate is measured over 60 days. Reply to every message within 60 minutes when possible. Even a short "Thanks, I'll review your request and respond in detail shortly" counts as a response and keeps your rate high.

⚡ Tip 2: Deliver Early, Always

Always deliver 12–24 hours before your deadline. Late deliveries kill your on-time rate AND your review score. Add extra buffer time when setting your delivery date — promise 5 days, deliver in 3.

⚡ Tip 3: Ask for Reviews (The Right Way)

After delivering, include a friendly note: "I hope this exceeds your expectations! If you're satisfied, a positive review really helps my small business grow. Thank you!" Never demand reviews or offer incentives — that violates Fiverr's TOS.

⚡ Tip 4: Use Buyer Requests Daily

In your early days, check Fiverr's "Buyer Requests" section every morning. Send 10 personalized proposals daily. New sellers who actively respond to buyer requests land their first orders 3x faster than those who just wait.

⚡ Tip 5: Never Cancel Orders

Order cancellations hurt your completion rate significantly. Before accepting an order, clarify all requirements. If an issue arises, communicate professionally and offer revisions before considering cancellation. A 95%+ completion rate is essential for level maintenance.

⚡ Tip 6: Optimize Gig SEO

Use keywords buyers search for in your gig title, description, and tags. Research: go to Fiverr search, type your service, look at what autocompletes. Use those exact phrases in your gig. Updated Fiverr algorithm in 2026 heavily weights keyword relevance.

⚡ Tip 7: Add a Gig Video

Gigs with a video get 220% more impressions and 40% more clicks. Record a 30–90 second video introducing yourself and your service. Even a simple screen recording with your voice-over explaining your gig is better than no video.

⚡ Tip 8: Price Strategy for New Sellers

Start with lower prices ($5–$20) to get your first 10 reviews quickly. Once you have social proof (reviews + level badge), raise prices to market rate. Don't stay cheap — buyers associate low prices with low quality after a point.

⚡ Tip 9: Create 7 Different Gigs Immediately

New sellers can create up to 7 gigs. Create all 7 on Day 1 — test different niches and see what gets impressions and clicks. After 2 weeks, identify which 2–3 gigs perform best and focus your energy there.

⚡ Tip 10: Share Your Gig Outside Fiverr

Share gig links on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Reddit (in relevant subreddits), Facebook groups, and TikTok. Fiverr rewards gigs that get external traffic with higher search rankings. External traffic = faster level progression.

9. Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How long does it take to reach Level 1?
Minimum 60 days. But if you actively use buyer requests, optimize your gigs, and deliver excellent work, most motivated sellers reach Level 1 in 60–90 days. The 10 orders requirement is usually the binding constraint — focus on getting orders first.
❓ Can I reach Level 2 without the $2,000 earnings?
No. The $2,000 total earnings is a hard requirement for Level 2. If you complete 50 orders at average $40 each, that's $2,000. Price your services accordingly — don't do all 50 orders at $5.
❓ What happens if I drop below the metrics requirements?
Fiverr evaluates levels on the 15th of each month. If you drop below requirements (e.g., response rate falls to 85%), you may be demoted a level. Maintain all metrics above 90% consistently to keep your level.
❓ Is Top Rated Seller really worth it?
Absolutely. Top Rated Sellers earn on average 4–6x more than Level 1 sellers. The TRS badge is the most powerful trust signal on Fiverr — buyers are much more willing to spend $200+ with a TRS than with a Level 1 seller charging the same for the same service.
❓ What are the most popular gig categories on Fiverr in 2026?
Top categories: AI Services (prompt writing, AI image generation, AI video), Short-Form Video Editing (Reels/TikTok/Shorts), Content Writing, Logo & Brand Design, WordPress Development, Social Media Management, and Voice Over. AI-related gigs are the fastest growing in 2026.
❓ Can I offer AI-generated work on Fiverr?
Yes, but you must disclose it in your gig description. Many successful gigs are explicitly "AI-assisted" or "AI-generated" services. Buyers specifically look for these. The key is transparency — don't sell AI work as human work without disclosure.
