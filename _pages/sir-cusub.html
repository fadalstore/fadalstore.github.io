1. AI Prompt Waa Maxay — Si Fudud oo Aad Fahamto

Marka aad u tagto ChatGPT oo aad ku qorto "Ii qor warqad" — taasi waa prompt aad u fudud. Laakiin haddaad ku qorto:

📌 Tusaale: Prompt Caadi ah vs Prompt Xirfad leh

Prompt caadi ah: "Ii qor maqaal ganacsiga ku saabsan"

Prompt xirfad leh ($15 qiimihiisu yahay):
"Act as a senior business consultant with 20 years of experience. Write a compelling 1,200-word blog article about [TOPIC] for small business owners. Include: a hook opening, 5 actionable strategies with real-world examples, a common mistake to avoid, and a motivating conclusion. Use a conversational yet authoritative tone. Format with H2 and H3 subheadings. Target audience: entrepreneurs making $0-$100k annually."

Aragtaa farqiga? Prompt-ga labaad wuxuu ChatGPT ka dhigayaa khabiir gaar ah oo natiijo aad u wanaagsan soo saara. Dadku waxay bixinayaan $5–$50 si ay u helaan prompts-yadaas wanaagsan — mana rabin inay waqti ku lumiyaan la tijaabinaya.

4. Sida Aad Prompt Fiican u Qorto — Tallaabooyin Fudud

Prompt wanaagsan wuxuu leeyahay qaab gaar ah. Halkan waa formula-da:

📌 Formula-da Prompt-ka Lacag leh

  1. [DOORASHO / ROLE] — U sheeg AI-ga inuu noqdo khabiir gaar ah
    Tusaale: "Act as a professional copywriter with 15 years experience"
  2. [SHAQADA] — Si faahfaahsan u sheeg waxa aad rabtid
    Tusaale: "Write a 500-word sales email for [PRODUCT]"
  3. [XAALADAHA] — Ku dar shuruudaha gaar ah
    Tusaale: "Include: subject line, opening hook, 3 benefits, social proof, and CTA"
  4. [DHAGEYSIGA] — U sheeg cidda loogu qorayo
    Tusaale: "Audience: small business owners aged 30-50"
  5. [QAABKA] — Sidee u soo saaro
    Tusaale: "Use bullet points, keep paragraphs short, avoid jargon"

Prompt-ga aad qortid, tixgeli oo tixgeli si aad u heshid mid xirfadlayaasha ah. Ka dibna ku iibso PromptBase ama Gumroad.

✅ Tusaale Dakhli Xaqiiq ah

8. Su'aalaha Badan La Is Waydiiya

❓ Ma u baahan tahay aqoon gaar ah si aad prompt u qorto?
Maya! Aqoon gaar ah kuma baahantid. Waxa aad u baahan tahay waa inay aad u fahansan tahay waxa dadku u baahan yihiin, oo aad prompt-gaaga si xirfadlayaasha ah u qorto. Markaad tijaabiso oo aad barato, waxay noqonaysaa wax fudud.
❓ ChatGPT ma bilaash buu yahay?
ChatGPT wuxuu leeyahay qaybta bilaashka ah (GPT-3.5) oo aad bilaabi karto. GPT-4o (Plus, $20/bil) waa mid ka wanaagsan, laakiin bilow heerka bilaashka ah si aad u baranayso. DALL-E iyo Midjourney waxay bixinayaan free trials si aad u tixgelinayso.
❓ Prompts-kayga ma qof kale ayuu koobiyayn karaa?
Haa, marka qof iibsado, wuxuu heli karaa prompts-kaaga. Laakiin sirrka waa in aad si joogto ah u samayso prompts cusub oo ku salaysan xogtaada taariikheed. PromptBase waxay sidoo kale leedahay policy xaddidaysa dib-u-iibinta.
❓ Imisa waqti buu qaataa intaan iibsi helo?
Inta badan, toddobaad 2–4 buu qaataa si aad iibiyo heshid — haddaad xayeysiis ka samaysatid. Haddaadan xayeysiis lahayn, waxay noqon kartaa 1–3 bilood. Dejinta TikTok ama YouTube video ah waxay si weyn u degdejisaa.
❓ Amazon KDP ma loo qaban karaa jooga Soomaaliya?
Haa, laakiin diiwaangelinta waxay u baahan tahay cinwaan UK, US, ama EU ah si lacagta loo qaato. Qaar ka mid ah Soomaali ah waxay isticmaalaan cinwaan qaraabada dibadda ku nool. Beddelkeedna isticmaal Gumroad ama Payhip — labadaba kuugu fudud yihiin Soomaaliya.
❓ Nooca prompts-yada xagee laga ogaan karaa in dadku u baahan yihiin?
Isticmaal: PromptBase si aad u aragto prompts-yada ugu badan iibsada (filter by "Best Selling"), Reddit (r/ChatGPT, r/midjourney), iyo Google Trends. Raadi waxa dadku waydiistaan oo aad u samayso prompt-ga xalinta dhibaatadaas.
