4. How to Install WordPress on Hostinger

WordPress is the world's most popular website platform, used by over 43% of all websites. It is free, powerful, and easy to use. Here is how to install it on your Hostinger hosting in 2 minutes:

  1. Log in to hPanel
    Go to hostinger.com and click "Log In." Enter your email and password to access your control panel.
  2. Go to "Websites"
    In the hPanel dashboard, click on the "Websites" section. You will see your domain listed there.
  3. Click "Manage" → "Auto Installer"
    Find the Auto Installer option. Click on the WordPress icon to begin the automatic installation.
  4. Fill in your WordPress details
    Enter your website title, admin username, and password. Make sure to write down your username and password somewhere safe. Click "Install."
  5. Wait 1–2 minutes
    Hostinger will automatically install WordPress. When finished, you will see a success message with your WordPress admin URL (usually yoursite.com/wp-admin).
  6. Log in to WordPress
    Go to yoursite.com/wp-admin and enter the username and password you created. Welcome to your WordPress dashboard — your website is ready to customize!
