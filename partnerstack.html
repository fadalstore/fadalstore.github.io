1. What Is PartnerStack?

PartnerStack (formerly GrowSumo) is the world's largest dedicated B2B SaaS partner ecosystem and affiliate marketplace. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Toronto, Canada, it has grown to become the go-to platform for software companies looking to scale through affiliate partnerships — and for content creators and marketers looking to earn premium recurring commissions.

Unlike traditional affiliate networks like ShareASale, Commission Junction, or Amazon Associates, PartnerStack is exclusively focused on software and SaaS (Software as a Service) products. This specialization is the key reason it offers dramatically better commission rates and terms than general affiliate networks.

Think about it this way: when you recommend a $20 physical product on Amazon, you might earn $1–$2 (5–10%). But when you recommend a SaaS tool that costs $50–$500 per month, you can earn 20–30% recurring commissions every single month the customer stays subscribed. That one referral could earn you $10–$150 per month, month after month, for years.

The PartnerStack Ecosystem

PartnerStack operates as a two-sided marketplace:

  • For Companies (Vendors): SaaS businesses use PartnerStack to recruit, manage, and pay affiliates, resellers, and referral partners at scale.
  • For Partners (Affiliates like you): Content creators, bloggers, YouTubers, email marketers, and anyone with an audience can join PartnerStack, discover programs, and start promoting SaaS products for commissions.

As of 2026, PartnerStack hosts over 800 partner programs from companies ranging from tiny startups to publicly traded corporations. The platform has paid out over $1 billion in total commissions to partners globally — and that number is growing fast.

The SaaS industry is projected to reach $1.2 trillion by 2030. Every business — from solopreneurs to Fortune 500 companies — needs software tools. That means the demand for the products you'll be promoting is massive and growing every year.

2. Why SaaS Affiliate Marketing Pays More Than Anything Else

Before diving deeper into PartnerStack, it's important to understand why SaaS affiliate marketing is fundamentally different from — and better than — most other forms of affiliate marketing.

Recurring Commissions: The True Power

Most affiliate programs pay you a one-time commission when someone makes a purchase. SaaS affiliate programs typically pay you every single month for as long as your referral remains a customer. This creates passive income that compounds over time.

📊 Traditional vs. SaaS Affiliate Income Comparison

Traditional Product (one-time, $100 purchase, 10%)$10 once
SaaS Tool ($100/month subscription, 25% recurring)$25/month
After 12 months (same single referral)$300 vs $10
After 3 years (same single referral)$900 vs $10

Now multiply that by 100 or 500 referrals and you start to see the explosive income potential. Top PartnerStack partners earn $10,000–$100,000+ per month purely from recurring SaaS commissions built up over time.

High-Value Products, High Commissions

SaaS products are often essential business tools that companies cannot easily stop using once integrated. A business might use an accounting tool, CRM, email marketing platform, and project management software every single day. Churn rates for established SaaS are low — meaning your commissions keep coming month after month.

Commission rates on PartnerStack typically range from 15% to 50% of the subscription value. Some programs offer even higher rates, especially for annual plans. A few premium programs pay flat bonuses of $200–$500 per qualified referral.

B2B Customers Spend More

Business software buyers are not bargain hunters. They're making purchasing decisions based on ROI, not price sensitivity. A business that subscribes to a project management tool at $200/month is happy to pay it because it saves them thousands in productivity. This means higher average subscription values and higher commissions for you.

3. How PartnerStack Works: Step-by-Step

PartnerStack's mechanics are simple and transparent. Here's exactly how the platform works from signup to your first commission payout:

  1. Create Your Free Account: Sign up at PartnerStack using this invite link. The signup process takes about 5 minutes. You'll provide basic information about yourself and your promotional channels (website, YouTube, social media, email list, etc.).
  2. Browse the Program Marketplace: Once logged in, you'll see the Partner Programs marketplace with 800+ programs organized by category, commission rate, cookie duration, and more. You can search and filter to find programs that match your niche and audience.
  3. Apply to Programs: Apply to programs that interest you. Some programs auto-approve partners instantly. Others require a brief review (1–3 days). Once approved, you get access to your unique affiliate links and promotional materials.
  4. Promote and Drive Traffic: Share your unique affiliate links through your content channels — blog posts, YouTube videos, social media, email newsletters, podcasts, or any other platform. PartnerStack tracks all clicks and conversions through your unique tracking code.
  5. Earn Commissions: When someone clicks your link and subscribes to the product, PartnerStack records the conversion. You earn a commission based on the program's terms. For recurring programs, you continue earning every month the customer remains subscribed.
  6. Get Paid: PartnerStack pays partners via PayPal, Stripe, or bank transfer (depending on your location). Most programs pay monthly, with a typical minimum payout threshold of $5–$25. Payments are processed reliably and on schedule.
PartnerStack's cookie duration is typically 30–90 days — sometimes longer. This means if someone clicks your link and doesn't buy immediately, you'll still get credit if they purchase within the cookie window.

The Partner Dashboard

Your PartnerStack dashboard shows you everything in one place: active programs you've joined, total clicks, conversion rates, pending commissions, and scheduled payouts. The interface is clean, intuitive, and updated in real-time. You can track exactly which programs and links are performing best and optimize accordingly.

7. Proven Strategies to Maximize Your PartnerStack Earnings

Strategy 1: Focus on Recurring Commission Programs First

Build your foundation on programs with recurring commissions. Even if a program pays a lower rate than a one-time bonus program, the long-term value is almost always higher. A $25/month recurring commission from a single referral is worth $300/year and potentially thousands over the customer's lifetime.

Strategy 2: Promote Tools You Actually Use

Authenticity converts. The affiliate marketers who earn the most on PartnerStack genuinely use the products they promote. When you can share real screenshots, real results, and honest assessments, your audience trusts your recommendations — and trust leads to conversions.

Strategy 3: Target "Best Software" and "Review" Keywords

People who search for "[Product Name] review" or "best [category] software" are in active buying mode. These keywords convert at 5–15% compared to 0.5–2% for informational content. Building a library of SEO-optimized review and comparison articles targeting buyer-intent keywords is the most reliable path to consistent affiliate income.

Strategy 4: Build an Email List Alongside Your Affiliate Content

Your email list is your most valuable affiliate asset. When you recommend a tool to your email subscribers, conversion rates can reach 5–20%. A list of 5,000 engaged subscribers can generate thousands in affiliate income from a single well-crafted email campaign.

Strategy 5: Stack Multiple Programs in Related Niches

Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Promote 5–15 programs across related categories. For example, if you create content for small business owners, you might promote tools for accounting (QuickBooks/FreshBooks), email marketing (ActiveCampaign), project management (Monday.com), and customer support (Freshworks).

Strategy 6: Create Comparison Content

"QuickBooks vs FreshBooks," "Canva vs Adobe Express," "ActiveCampaign vs Mailchimp" — comparison articles and videos are extremely powerful because they capture buyers who are already 80% ready to purchase and just need to pick between options. Include affiliate links for both products and you earn no matter which one they choose.

8. Best Content Types for Promoting PartnerStack Products

1. In-Depth Software Reviews (Highest Converting)

A detailed 2,000–5,000 word review covering features, pricing, pros and cons, real use cases, and who it's best for. Include screenshots, personal experience, and a clear recommendation. This type of content ranks well on Google and converts at high rates because readers arrive with strong purchase intent.

2. "Best Of" Roundup Articles

"Best email marketing tools for small businesses," "Top 10 project management apps for remote teams" — these articles capture broad keyword traffic and allow you to include multiple affiliate links in a single piece of content. Include 5–10 tools with your affiliate links for each.

3. Tutorial and How-To Content

Show people how to accomplish something using a specific tool: "How to set up email automation in ActiveCampaign," "How to create a professional logo with Canva," "How to manage your books with QuickBooks." This content attracts people who are actively evaluating a tool and positions you as a trusted expert.

4. YouTube Review Videos

Video reviews generate high conversion rates because viewers can see the product in action. A 10–15 minute walkthrough of a tool's features with an honest assessment can generate significant affiliate income. Include your affiliate link prominently in the video description.

5. Email Sequences

A 5–7 email welcome sequence introducing new subscribers to the tools you recommend and use is one of the highest-ROI activities in affiliate marketing. When someone trusts you enough to give you their email, they're far more likely to act on your recommendations.

6. Case Studies

Document your own results using a tool: "How I grew my email list by 3,000 subscribers using ActiveCampaign," "How I saved 5 hours per week using Zapier automation." Real case studies with specific numbers are extremely compelling and convert at very high rates.

11. Advanced Tips for Power Partners

Tip 1: Negotiate Better Rates

Once you've been generating consistent referrals for a program for 3–6 months, reach out to the program manager and ask about a rate increase. Many companies are happy to offer higher commissions to their top-performing partners. This alone can increase your monthly income by 20–50% without any additional work.

Tip 2: Build Relationships with Program Managers

Most programs on PartnerStack have a dedicated partner manager. Introduce yourself, ask about exclusive promotions or beta access to new features, and stay in regular communication. Well-connected affiliates get early access to high-converting promotions and seasonal deals that drive significant income spikes.

Tip 3: Optimize for Page Speed and User Experience

If you're driving traffic through a website, ensure your pages load in under 2 seconds on mobile. Slow pages lose 40–60% of potential visitors before they even read your affiliate content. Use Google PageSpeed Insights to check your scores and optimize images, eliminate unnecessary scripts, and enable browser caching.

Tip 4: Build a Resource Page on Your Website

A "Tools I Use and Recommend" resource page is a passive commission goldmine. Once you've built an audience, this page can generate hundreds in recurring monthly commissions with virtually zero maintenance. Simply list the tools you recommend with brief descriptions and your affiliate links.

Tip 5: Track and Attribute Correctly

Use UTM parameters to track which specific pieces of content, emails, or social posts are generating the most conversions. When you know your best-performing content formats and distribution channels, you can replicate success systematically rather than guessing.

Tip 6: Diversify Your Income with Multiple Programs

Never rely on a single affiliate program. Programs can change their commission structure, pause their affiliate program, or even shut down. Maintaining active relationships with 10–20 programs across different categories protects your income and creates multiple growth opportunities.

12. Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is PartnerStack free to join?
Yes, PartnerStack is completely free for affiliate partners (people who want to promote products and earn commissions). There are no signup fees, no monthly fees, and no minimum earnings requirements. Companies (vendors) pay to list their programs on PartnerStack, but partners never pay anything.
❓ How much can I realistically earn on PartnerStack?
This varies enormously based on your audience size, niche, content quality, and promotional consistency. Beginners who actively create content typically earn $100–$500/month within 2–3 months. After 6–12 months of consistent effort, $1,000–$5,000/month is realistic for most niches. Advanced partners with established audiences earn $10,000–$100,000+/month.
❓ Do I need a website to join PartnerStack?
No, you don't need a website. PartnerStack accepts partners who use YouTube channels, email newsletters, LinkedIn, podcasts, social media accounts, or other promotional channels. However, having a website significantly increases your earning potential because SEO content generates traffic 24/7 without ongoing promotion effort.
❓ How does PartnerStack pay its partners?
PartnerStack pays partners via PayPal and Stripe for most regions. Bank transfer (ACH) is also available in some countries. Most programs pay monthly, typically on the 13th or 15th of each month for the previous month's commissions. Minimum payout thresholds vary by program but are typically between $5–$25.
❓ Can I join PartnerStack if I'm outside the US?
Yes, PartnerStack is a global platform. Partners from most countries can join and participate in the majority of programs. Some programs may have geographic restrictions, but most accept partners from Africa, Asia, Europe, Latin America, and the Middle East. Payment via PayPal is available in virtually all countries where PayPal operates.
❓ How long do PartnerStack cookies last?
Cookie duration varies by program, but most programs offer 30–90 day cookies. Some programs offer longer or even indefinite attribution (meaning you get credit for a customer for their entire lifetime on the platform). Compare this to Amazon Associates' 24-hour cookie, and you can see why PartnerStack is so much more favorable for affiliates.
❓ What niches work best on PartnerStack?
PartnerStack works best for creators in business, marketing, technology, finance, productivity, design, e-commerce, HR, and any other niche where professionals use software tools. The sweet spot is any content that speaks to small business owners, freelancers, marketers, developers, or entrepreneurs — all of whom are heavy software users with purchasing budgets.
❓ How quickly can I get approved for programs?
Many programs on PartnerStack offer instant auto-approval, meaning you get access to your affiliate links immediately after applying. More competitive or exclusive programs may take 1–5 business days for a manual review. Having a complete profile and established promotional channels (website with some content, social media presence, etc.) significantly improves your approval rate for manual review programs.
