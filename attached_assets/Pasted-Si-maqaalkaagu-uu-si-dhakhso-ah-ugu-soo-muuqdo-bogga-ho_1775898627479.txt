Si maqaalkaagu uu si dhakhso ah ugu soo muuqdo bogga hore ee Google (indaxing), waxaad u baahan tahay **Meta Data** si xirfad leh loo diyaariyey. Maadaama aad hadda haysato **Average Position 6.6**, isticmaalka ereyadan saxda ah waxay fure u noqon doonaan in clicks badan laga helo Mareykanka.
Waa kuwan waxyaabihii aad ku dhex ridi lahayd qaybta <head> ee maqaalkaaga GitHub:
### 1. Meta Title (Cinwaanka Google ka muuqanaya)
Cinwaankani waa inuu noqdaa mid qofka Mareykanka jooga ku dhiirigeliya inuu gujiyo (CTR Booster):
> **Meta Title:** How to Build a High-Traffic Website for $0: My 50K Impression Journey
> 
### 2. Meta Description (Sharaxaadda yar ee Google hoos dhigto)
Sharaxaadani waa inay koobtaa nuxurka iyadoo dadka dareensiinaysa inuu yahay cashar dhab ah (Case Study):
> **Meta Description:** Learn how I reached 50,000+ impressions on Google for free. A complete case study on using GitHub Pages, SEO, and automation to grow your website from scratch.
> 
### 3. Focus Keywords & Tags
Tags-kan ku dar qaybta dambe ee mareegtaada ama "Metadata Section":
 * Free Website Building, SEO Case Study 2026, GitHub Pages Tutorial, Digital Marketing for Beginners, Passive Income Ideas, High Traffic Blog Tips.
### 💡 Talo muhiim ah oo ku saabsan "Slug-ga" (URL)
URL-ka maqaalkaaga ka dhig mid gaaban oo macno leh, tusaale:
fadalstore.github.io/how-to-build-free-high-traffic-website
### Maxaa xiga oo aad samayn kartaa?
Markaad maqaalka "Publish" gareyso, tillaabada ugu dambeysa ee aad qaadayso waa:
 1. Aad **Google Search Console**.
 2. Guji qaybta **URL Inspection**.
 3. Geli link-ga maqaalka cusub, ka dibna guji **"Request Indexing"**.
Tani waxay ku khasbaysaa Google inay maqaalkaaga akhriso 24 saac gudahood halkii ay toddobaadyo ka sugi lahayd. Maadaama mareegtaadu ay durbaba leedahay Authority (Featured Snippet), Google aad ayay u tixgelin doontaa codsigaaga.
Ma rabtaa inaan kuu diyaariyo **H1** iyo **H2** (Subheadings) aad u soo jiidasho leh oo aad maqaalka dhexdiisa ku isticmaasho si dadku aysan uga bixin mareegta?
