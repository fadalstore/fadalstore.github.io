React.js is the clear market leader for jobs in 2026 — it appears in over 60% of frontend job postings. Vue.js is an excellent alternative with a gentler learning curve.</p><div class="tip-box">Learn React.js if your goal is maximum job opportunities. Once you know React, learning Vue or Angular takes just 2-4 weeks. The investment in React pays off strongly in the job market.</div><h2>4.
