Haddaad bilowga ah tahay freelancing-ga oo aad is weydiineyso **"Fiverr mise Upwork?"** — jawaabtu waa: labaduba waa fiican, laakiin **mid kasta wuxuu u fiican yahay dad gaar ah.**

Maqaalkan waxaan ku sharxayaa xaqiiqda dhabta ah — dadka dakhliga online-ka ka sameeysta ee aan la hadlay ayaan ku saleelay.

---

## Fiverr — Waa Maxay?

**Fiverr** waa marketplace ah oo aad ku "sameyso gig" — macaamiliin ayaa kula soo xidhiidha. Adiga ma aadan u baahnin inaad shaqo codsato — macmiilku ayaa adiga ku heli doona.

### ✅ Faa'iidooyinka Fiverr
- **Bilaab $5** — xitaa macmiiliin aad u yar ayaa $5 ka bilaaba
- **Macaamiliin ayaa kugu yimaada** — profile samee oo sug
- **Bilowga ugu habboon** — khibrad laguma baahna
- **Dhamaan xirfadaha** — video, qorista, design, cod, tarjumad, coding

### ❌ Xadidaadka Fiverr
- Fiverr wuxuu kaa qaadaa **20% commission** hadba lacagta aad heshid
- Tartanka — seller badan, gaar ahaan qiimaha hoose
- Marka bilowga ah, macmiil heli waa adag tahay (ilaa aad review-yo heshid)

### 💰 Lacagta Fiverr
- Bilowga: **$5–$50** mashruuc kasta
- 6 bilood ka dib: **$200–$1,000** biloodkiiba (haddaad xirfad wanaagsan leedahay)
- Khibradda leh: **$2,000–$10,000+** biloodkiiba

**→ [Fiverr account bilaash samee — halkan gal](https://www.fiverr.com)**

---

## Upwork — Waa Maxay?

**Upwork** waa platform ah oo adigu shaqo codsato — client-yadu job posting ayay daabacaan, adigu **proposal** ayaad u diraysaa.

### ✅ Faa'iidooyinka Upwork
- **Lacag badan** — $20–$150/saacad clients badan way bixiyaan
- **Shaqooyin dhaadheer** — contracts dheer oo biloodba lacag joogto ah keena
- **Macaamiliin ganacsi weyn** — shirkadaha weyn badan bay Upwork isticmaalaan
- **Hourly tracking** — saacadda shaqada waa la xaqiijiyaa, lacag la'aani ma jirto

### ❌ Xadidaadka Upwork
- **Connects** — Upwork wuxuu u baahan yahay "connects" (credits) si aad proposal u dirtid
- **Adag in la galo** — profile cusub oo reviewed la'aanta ah waa adag tahay in la ogolaaday
- **Commission 20%** — ilaa aad $500 macmiil kaaga heshid, 20% ayuu qaadaa

### 💰 Lacagta Upwork
- Bilowga: **$10–$25/saacad**
- 3–6 bilood ka dib: **$30–$60/saacad**
- Khibradda leh: **$60–$150/saacad**

**→ [Upwork account bilaash samee — halkan gal](https://www.upwork.com)**

## Midkee Aad Ka Bilaabdo?

### 🟢 Fiverr — haddaad tahay:
- **Cusub buuxa** oo aan khibrad lahayn
- **Design, video, tarjumad, qorista** isticmaalaya
- Macaamiliin adiga u imaadaan rabta
- Gig **gaara** sameyso oo specialization ku samee

### 🔵 Upwork — haddaad tahay:
- **Khibrad leedahay** oo portfolio ku leedahay
- **Developer, accountant, project manager** noocahiis
- Shaqo dheer oo dakhli joogto ah rabtaa
- Ingiriis si wanaagsan u hadasha

---

## Xeelada Ugu Fiican — Labadaba Isticmaal

Freelancers-ka dakhliga ugu badan sameysta **labadaba waxay isticmaalaan:**

1. **Fiverr** — gig passive ah samee, macaamiliin u imaadaan
2. **Upwork** — maalin kasta 3–5 proposal dir shaqooyinka cusub

Tani waxay kuu samaynaysaa "income stream" laba ah — mid passive (Fiverr) iyo mid active (Upwork).
