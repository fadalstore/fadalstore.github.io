## Waxa Wanaagsan — ✅ Faa'iidooyinka

### 1. WordPress 1-Minute Setup
Marka aad account samayneyso, "WordPress" ayaad gujisaa oo 1 daqiiqo gudahood website-kaagu wuu diyaaraa. Codding laguma baahna.

### 2. Speed Wanaagsan
Hostinger wuxuu isticmaalaa **LiteSpeed servers** iyo **CloudFlare CDN** — website-kaagu aad buu u dhakhsaa. PageSpeed Insights guduhu 85–95 ayuu maraa.

### 3. hPanel — Aad u Fudud
Control panel-ka Hostinger (hPanel) waa mid aad u fudud — cPanel oo la fududeeyay. Aad u habboon bilowga.

### 4. SSL Bilaash
HTTPS/SSL certificate bilaash ayuu kuu siin yahay — Google-ka lagaga baahan yahay website-yada.

### 5. Domain Bilaash (Sanadka 1aad)
Premium iyo Business plans-yada waxay kugu siiyaan **domain name bilaash** sanadka ugu horeeysa.

## Xadidaadka — ❌ Waxa Aan Fiicnayn

### 1. Qiimaha Cusbooneysiinta
Sanadka 1aad qiimaha hoose — ka dib **2–3 jeer** ayuu kor u kacaa. Hubi ka hor iibsashada.

### 2. Customer Support
Support-ka waa 24/7, laakiin live chat kaliya ayaa jira — phone ma jirto. Weydimaha fudud ayaa dhakhso la xalliyaa, kuwa adag waxay qaadanayaan 2–4 saacad.

### 3. Backup Daily
Daily backup oo automatic ah — **Business plan** kaliya. Single iyo Premium waxay siiyaan weekly backup.
