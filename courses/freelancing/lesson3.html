Casharkaan: Fiverr iyo Upwork labadoodaba waa muhiim. Upwork wuxuu bixiyaa shaqooyin lacag badan leh — laakiin wuxuu u baahan yahay xirfad proposal qorista. Casharkaan waxaad baraneyso sida profile adag loo sameeyo iyo sida proposal guuleysta loo qoro.

Upwork Account Samee

  1. Aad upwork.com oo "Sign Up as a Talent" dooro
  2. Category-gaaga xulo (Writing, Design, Development, Marketing, iwm)
  3. Xirfadahaada ku dar (3–5 xirfadood)
  4. Sawirkaaga iyo bio-gaaga ku dar
  5. Heerka waayo-aragnimadaada xulo (Entry, Intermediate, Expert)
Muhiim: Upwork wuxuu dib u eegaa profile-yada cusub. Marka aad dhammaystirto profile-kaaga, waxay u baahan yihiin 24–48 saacadood si ay u ansixiyaan. Ka samee profile-kaaga 100% dhamaystiran si aad si dhakhso ah uga geli karto.

Profile Adag — Waxa Muhiimka ah

Professional Photo

Sawir cad, weji muujinaya, dhar xirfad muujinaysa. Shirkadaha badan waxay arkaan sawirkaaga kahor inta aysan akhriinin profile-kaaga.

Title-kaaga (Professional Title)

Ha qorin wax caadi ah. Noqo gaar ah:

  • ❌ "Freelancer" — caadi, macno la'aan
  • ✅ "SEO Content Writer | Blog Articles That Rank on Google"
  • ✅ "Video Editor | Fast Delivery | YouTube & Social Media"
  • ✅ "English-Somali Translator | Native Speaker | Fast & Accurate"

Overview (Sharaxaadda)

Overview-gaaga waa inuu ka jawaabaa:

  • Cidda aad tahay iyo xirfadahaada
  • Macaamiliin noocee la shaqeysay (haddaad lahayd)
  • Natiijada aad bixineyso (ma adeeg — natiijo)
  • Sida lagula soo xidhiidhi karo

Proposals — Sida Guuleysta Loo Qoro

Proposal waa warqadda aad macmiilka u dirto marka aad shaqo codsaneyso. Proposals badan ayaa fashilmaa sababtoo ah waxay si caadi ah u bilaabanayaan.

Khaladaadka Sida Badanka

  • "I am a professional freelancer with 5 years experience..." — macmiilka ma daneynayso cidda aad tahay, wuxuu daneynayaa sida uu kaa faa'iideysan karo
  • Proposal la koobi karay — macaamiliin waxay garaan haddaad koobi/paste garayso
  • Dheer aad — macaamiliin busy ayay yihiin

Qaabka Proposal-ka Guuleysta

Hi [Name],

I noticed you need [specific thing from their job post]. I've done exactly this for [similar project/client type].

Here's how I'd approach your project:
1. [Step 1 — specific to their job]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Delivery — timeline]

A quick question before I start: [one relevant question about their project]

I can start immediately and deliver within [timeline].

[Your name]
Sababta Su'aalaha Muhiim u Tahay: Marka aad weydiiyo su'aal gaara oo ku saabsan mashruucahooda, waxay arkaan inaaad akhriday job posting-kooda si taxadar leh. Tani waxay kuu sameysaa inaa ka duwan tahay 90% freelancers-ka kale oo template isticmaalaya.

Connects — Budget-ka Upwork

Upwork wuxuu isticmaalaa "Connects" — mid kasta oo proposal ah wuxuu kaa qaadaa 6–12 connects. Account cusub wuxuu kuu siinayaa 80 connects bilaash ah.

  • Codso shaqooyinka aad ku guuleysan karto — ha ku lumin connects shaqooyinka aad ogid inaanad helin
  • Shaqooyinka cusub ee posted waxay helaan proposals yar — codso 24 saacadood gudahood
  • Shaqooyinka budget heer dhexe leh ($100–$500) waxaa ka yar tartanka marka loo eego kuwa $1,000+
Samir Ku yeelo: Upwork-ka bilowga waa xoog. Freelancers badan waxay helaan 10–20 rejection ka hor intayanay helin shaqadii ugu horeysay. Taasi caadi. Ku sii dar — marka aad 2–3 reviews heshid, shaqada ay hesho waa fududaataa si weyn.
