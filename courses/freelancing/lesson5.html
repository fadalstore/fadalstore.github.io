Casharka 5 / 6 — Freelancing

Sida Lacagta Loogu Qaato
Payoneer, Wise & Bank Transfer

Xalinta su'aasha ugu muhiimsan ee freelancer-yada Soomaalida

Hoyga › Koorsooyinka › Freelancing › Casharka 5
Su'aasha Ugu Badan: "Lacagta sidee baan ugu qaadan karaa Somalia/Eritrea/Ethiopia/Djibouti?" — Casharkaan wuxuu ka jawaabaa su'aalahan oo dhan si faahfaahsan.

Dhibaatada Asalka ah

Freelancers-ka badan ee Afrika Bariga waxay la kulmaan dhibaato mid: lacagta macaamilku bixiyo (caadi ahaan PayPal ama wire transfer) sida loogu keeno gacanta. Tani waxay u baahan tahay account dhex ah oo lacagta ku kaydsata.

Xalka ugu fiican waa inaad isticmaasho mid ka mid ah kuwan saddexda: Payoneer, Wise, ama Airtm.

1. Payoneer — Ugu Caansan Freelancers-ka

💳 Payoneer

Payoneer waa adeegga ugu caansan freelancers-ka adduunka oo dhan. Wuxuu ku siinayaa xisaab bangi ah oo Maraykanka ah (USD), Yurub (EUR), UK (GBP), iwm — adiga oo aan USA ku jirin.

✓ Fiverr iyo Upwork labadaba si toos ah ayuu ula shaqeeyaa
✓ Debit card (Mastercard) ayuu kuu soo dira — card-ka aad dukaamaha ku bixin karto
✓ Lacagta bank-ka local-ka ah ugu dir
✓ Somalia, Ethiopia, Kenya, Djibouti waxay ka shaqeeyaan
✗ Fee-yada waxay $3 ku yihiin transfer kasta
✗ Currency conversion fee waxay tahay 2–3%

Sida Payoneer Loo Sameeyo

  1. Aad payoneer.com oo "Sign Up" riix
  2. Macluumaadkaaga shaqsiga ku dar (magac, taariikhda dhalashada, cinwaanka)
  3. ID-gaaga (passport ama national ID) upload gare
  4. Xisaabta bankiga ee local-ka ku dar lacagta loogu diro
  5. Fiverr ama Upwork ku xidh: Settings → Billing → Add Payoneer
Payoneer Referral: Haddaad macmiil kaaga weydiso inuu ku soo diro lacagta Payoneer-ka tooska ah (G2G — Global to Global Payment), waxaad lacag baddan ka badbaadisaa. Fiverr iyo Upwork waxay qaadhaan 20% — Payoneer toos ah waa 1–2% kaliya.

2. Wise (TransferWise) — Ugu Jaban

🌍 Wise

Wise wuxuu bixinayaa xisaab bangi "multi-currency" ah — USD, EUR, GBP, AUD iwm. Fee-yadiisu waa kuwa ugu hooseeya adduunka.

✓ Ugu jaban currency conversion-ka (0.5–1.5%)
✓ Lacagta dhakhso loo helo (saacado)
✓ App wanaagsan oo fudud
✓ Debit card aad dukaamada online ku bixin karto
✗ Dhammaan wadamada ayay ka shaqeyneyso — hubi hadduu Somalia ka shaqeeyo
✗ Fiverr/Upwork si toos ah uguma xidna — waxaad u baahan tahay lacagta marka hore Payoneer ugu dirto

3. Airtm — Xalka Afrika Bariga

🔄 Airtm

Airtm waxaa loogu talagalay wadamada PayPal ka shaqeyneyso ee ay yar yihiin. Waxay kaa caawinaysaa lacagta aad ka hesho Fiverr/Upwork inaad u beddesho EVC Plus, Zaad, ama bangi local ah.

✓ Somalia iyo Somaliland waxay ka shaqeeyaan
✓ EVC Plus iyo Zaad toos ayuu ula shaqeeyaa
✓ P2P — lacagta cid kale ayaad uga iibsataa
✗ Fee-yada way sarreeyaan (3–5%)
✗ Waqti badan ayuu qaadaa marka loo eego Wise

Fiverr & Upwork — Lacagta Heerka

Muhiim — Canshuurta Taxes: Haddaad Maraykanka ku noolaatid ama sharikaad u shaqeyneyso Maraykanka ka socota, waxaad u baahan doontaa Form W-8BEN ama W-9. Fiverr iyo Upwork waxay kaa codsanayaan. Ha ka baqin — form-ka waa fudud, waa xog shaqsiga oo kaliya.

Xisaabta Baanka Local-ka ah

Marka lacagtu gaadhay Payoneer ama Wise, waxaad u diri kartaa baanka local-kaaga:

  • Somalia: Premier Bank, Salaam Bank (waxay ka helaan wire transfers)
  • Somaliland: Dahabshiil Bank, Premier Bank
  • Djibouti: Commercial Bank of Ethiopia, BCI
  • Kenya/Ethiopia: Baankooyinka caadiga ah oo dhan
