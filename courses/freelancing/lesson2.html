Fiverr — Account Samee &
Gig-kaaga Ugu Horeeyaa Qor

Casharkaan: Waxaad baraneyso sida Fiverr account loo sameeyo, profile adag loo dhiso, iyo gig (adeeg) kugu guuleysta sida loo qoro — si aad macaamiliin u hesho dhakhso.

Maxay Tahay Fiverr?

Fiverr waa platform internet ah oo macaamiliin (buyers) iyo freelancers (sellers) lagu xidho. Adigu waxaad soo dhigta "gig" — adeegga aad bixineyso — macaamiliin ayaa raadsanaya oo kula soo xiriira.

Fiverr-ka waxaa ka shaqeeya 4+ milyan oo freelancer ah adduunka oo dhan. Laakiin tani macnaheedu maaha in tartanku adag yahay — macaamiliin badan ayaana soo rabaa adeegyo kala duwan maalin walba.

Tallaabada 1: Account Samee

1

Fiverr.com u tag oo Sign Up riix

Email-kaaga isticmaal ama Google account. Xulo magac isticmaale (username) kuu eg xirfadahaada — tusaale: "designpro_hassan" ama "writer_faadumo".

2

Noqo Seller — "Become a Seller" riix

Account ku samee habka seller-ka. Fiverr wuxuu ku weydiin doonaa macluumaadkaaga shaqsiga, xirfadahaada, iyo waayo-aragnimadaada.

3

Profile Picture xidh

Sawir cad oo weji muujinaya. Fiverr-ka sawirrada aan wejiga muujineyn waxay heli karaan click yar. Sawirka waa muhiim — waa kalsoonida ugu horeysa ee macmiilka.

4

Bio qor (150–300 eray)

U sheeg macaamiliin cidda aad tahay, maxaad qaban karto, iyo sababta ay kuu dooran karaan. Ha isticmaalin erayo caadi ah — sheeg waxyaabaha gaarka ah ee kaa dhigaya mid gaar ah.

5

Xirfadahaada ku dar & Heer xulo

Ku dar xirfadahaada oo heerkooda (Beginner, Intermediate, Expert) calaamadee. Honest ahow — macaamiliin waxay arkaan hadaad xirfadda aad sheegto ku guulaysatid.

Tallaabada 2: Gig Ugu Horeeyaa Samee

Gig waa adeegga aad bixineyso. Marka aad sameyso gig wanaagsan, Fiverr wuxuu muujinaa macaamiliin raadsan adeegaas.

Cinwaanka Gig-ka (Title) — Ugu Muhiimsan

Cinwaanka waa waxa ugu muhiimsan ee gig-kaaga. Waa inuu:

  • Si cad u sharxaa waxa aad samayneyso
  • Ku jiro erayadii macaamiliin ka raadsan karaan
  • Natiijada uu macmiilku heli doono muujiyo
Tusaaleyaal Wanaagsan:
❌ "I will write articles" (caadi, aan gaara ahayn)
✅ "I will write SEO-optimized blog articles for your website"
✅ "I will professionally edit your YouTube video with captions"
✅ "I will translate your document from English to Somali or Arabic"

Qiimaha (Pricing) — Noqo Xiise leh

Bilowga, qiimaha hoose u dhig si aad reviews u hesho. Markaan jirin review, macaamiliin waxay aad ugu adkaataa inay kugu doorteen.

  • Basic: $5–$15 (shaqo fudud, dhakhso ah)
  • Standard: $20–$40 (shaqo dhexdhexaad ah)
  • Premium: $50–$100 (shaqo faahfaahsan, dheeraad ah)

Marka aad 5–10 reviews heshid, qiimaha kor u qaad si tartiib ah.

Sharaxaadda (Description)

Sharaxaadda gig-kaaga waa inay ka jawaabto su'aalahan:

  • Waxa macmiilku heli doono (natiijada go'an)
  • Sida shaqada loo qabto (hannaanka)
  • Waxa looga baahan yahay macmiilka (macluumaad, fayl, iwm)
  • Waqtiga dhammaystirka

Sawirrada iyo Portfolio

Ku dar sawir ama video oo muujinaya shaqadaada. Gigs-yada sawir leh waxay helaan 40% clicks badan kuwa aan lahayn marka loo eego. Haddaadan hore u lahayn shaqo, samee shaqo muunad ah oo muujisa xirfadahaada.

Khaladaadka Sida Badanka: Dadka badan waxay rabaanaan xukun (reviews) marka ay billaabaan. Xalka: weydii saaxiibbaadka ama qoyskaaga inay kula yeeshaan order yar oo aad muunad ugu sameyso. 5 reviews oo wanaagsan ayaa si weyn u wax uga baddalaya in macaamiliin kale la helo.

Gig-kaaga Ka Dib

Marka aad gig-kaaga soo dhigtid, waxa muhiimka ah:

  • 📨 Online joog: Fiverr wuxuu muujinaa seller-yada aad uga jawaabaya messages-ka. Jawaab 1 saacad gudahood haddaad kartid.
  • 🔄 Gig dib u cusboonaysii: Fiverr wuxuu kor u qaadaa gigs-yada la cusboonaysiiya. Toddobaadkiiba mar u cusboonaysii.
  • ⭐ Review codso: Marka shaqada dhammaato, si xushmad leh u weydii macmiilka inuu review kuu daayo.
